TIP: Hats are an easy way to either dress up or dress down an outfit, throw one on when you are having a bad hair day, wear for warmth, or just wear it for style.

Hats are a head covering worn for various reasons. The first hat was seen on a man in Egypt in 3200 BC. He was wearing a conical straw hat.

Thanks to some man in Egypt, we are lucky enough to wear various types of hats every day! No matter what your style is, there is most likely a hat to match it!
